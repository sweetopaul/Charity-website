<h2 class="title rbt-section-title"><span>Programme Vision (2018-2028)</span></h2>                            <p><b>Ignite: </b><br>
Bhumi will work towards providing holistic education and enabling sustainable livelihoods for 5,000 children in the next ten years.
<br>
For this we will focus on increasing impact and sustainability in shelter homes, communities and schools <br>
- The volunteer-driven programme at shelter homes will be strengthened for higher effectiveness. <br>
- The school programme will focus on improving student learning outcomes
<br>
- The communities programme will improve the community’s ownership on their children’s education <br>
<b>Catalyse: </b><br>
Bhumi will continue to strive to make volunteering a national habit. We will drive sustainable development goals through impactful volunteer programmes clocking crore hours in volunteering. <br>
Bhumi, as a platform, will groom at least 20% of volunteers as change leaders for society.</p>
